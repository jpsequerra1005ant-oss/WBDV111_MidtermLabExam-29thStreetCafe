About Us

Located in Marikina City, our business is dedicated to serving students from elementary to college, especially with our convenient location near schools. We are committed to providing quality products and excellent customer service, creating a welcoming space where students can easily access what they need. Our mission is to grow as a community-centered business that builds trust and customer loyalty through consistent service and reliable offerings.

To better serve our customers, we have developed a website that provides a centralized menu, real-time product availability, and reduces the need for repetitive inquiries on Messenger. This also helps us connect with the younger generation who are active online, allowing us to expand our reach and promote our business more effectively. Despite challenges such as strong competition from nearby coffee shops and well-known stores, as well as occasional slow sales, we remain focused on improving our services and strengthening our presence in the community.
